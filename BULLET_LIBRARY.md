## Bullet Writing Rules (read before filling in)
1. **Lead with impact, not activity.** "Grew X by 40%" not "Responsible for growing X."
2. **Every bullet needs a metric.** If you do not have an exact number, use a range or proxy.
3. **Under 40 words per bullet.** Hiring managers skim. Make every word earn its place.
4. **No em-dashes.** Use hyphens (-) or colons (:) instead. ATS systems sometimes choke on em-dashes.
5. **Preserve all numbers exactly.** Claude will never change metrics you enter here.
