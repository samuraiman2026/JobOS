Each answer scored on 3 dimensions:

Relevance - Did it answer what was actually asked?
Specificity - Was it concrete or vague?
Impact clarity - Did a metric or outcome land?

Target average: 4.0+/5 per answer.
Below 3.5 = drill this question type.
